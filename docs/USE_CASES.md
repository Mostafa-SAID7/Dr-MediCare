# Use Cases

Real-world scenarios and use cases for the Dr. MediCare platform.

## Patient Use Cases

### 1. First-Time Patient Registration

**Scenario**: Sarah is a new patient looking for a doctor.

**Steps**:
1. Visits the MediCare website
2. Browses available doctors by specialty
3. Reads doctor profiles and reviews
4. Creates a patient account
5. Books first appointment
6. Receives confirmation email

**Benefits**:
- Easy doctor discovery
- Transparent information
- Quick registration
- Instant booking confirmation

### 2. Regular Checkup Appointment

**Scenario**: John needs his annual physical examination.

**Steps**:
1. Logs into patient portal
2. Views his regular doctor's profile
3. Checks available time slots
4. Books appointment for next week
5. Adds to calendar
6. Receives reminder notification

**Benefits**:
- Familiar doctor relationship
- Convenient scheduling
- Automated reminders
- Calendar integration

### 3. Emergency Consultation

**Scenario**: Maria has sudden severe symptoms.
